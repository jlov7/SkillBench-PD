# Brand Voice Steward (Synthetic)

This Skill supports small marketing rewrites, policy formatting, and reporting summaries. It is designed for the SkillBench-PD benchmark.

## Use cases

### Rewrite brand copy
Keywords: rewrite, brand, tone, marketing
Steps:
1. Read the brief and note banned phrases.
2. Consult the brand voice guide: references/brand-voice.md
3. Produce a concise rewrite in active voice.

References:
- [Brand voice guide](references/brand-voice.md)

### Format policy snippets
Keywords: policy, format, bullet, compliance
Steps:
1. Convert the heading to sentence case.
2. Break each requirement into a clear bullet.
3. Preserve any numbers or absolutes exactly as written.

### Summarize metrics
Keywords: report, metrics, summary, data
Steps:
1. Identify the headline change and trend.
2. Consult the reporting style guide: references/reporting-style.md
3. Draft a three-bullet summary covering trend, impact, and next step.

References:
- [Reporting style guide](references/reporting-style.md)
